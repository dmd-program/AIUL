Sample Syllabus Language

The following templates can be adapted for use in your course syllabi:

General Policy Statement

AI Usage Policy: This course uses the AI Usage License (AIUL) tagging system to clearly communicate expectations around the use of AI tools in assignments. Each assignment will include an AIUL tag that indicates the permitted level of AI usage. Students are responsible for understanding and adhering to these guidelines. For more information about AIUL tags, please visit https://dmd-program.github.io/aiul.

Assignment-Specific Language

AIUL-1: This assignment is tagged AIUL-1, which means no AI tools are permitted. All work must be entirely your own without assistance from AI text generators, image generators, or similar tools.

AIUL-3-WR: This assignment is tagged AIUL-3-WR, which means you may use AI writing tools for research and idea generation, but the final written work must be entirely your own. You must cite any AI tools used in your research process.

AIUL-5-IM: This assignment is tagged AIUL-5-IM, which means you may use AI image generation tools to create your work, but you must provide clear direction to the AI, modify or refine the outputs, and document your process. The concept, direction, and post-processing should demonstrate your creative input.

Documentation Templates

For assignments with tags AIUL-3 through AIUL-6, we recommend requiring students to document their AI usage. The following templates can help structure this documentation:

AI Usage Documentation Template

AI Tools Used: [List all AI tools used in the process]

Purpose of AI Usage: [Describe how AI was integrated into your process]

Prompts/Inputs: [Document key prompts or inputs provided to AI tools]

Modifications: [Describe how you modified or refined AI outputs]

Reflection: [Reflect on the human-AI collaboration process, challenges, and insights]
